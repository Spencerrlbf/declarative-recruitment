01 — Who We Are

A specialist firm built for the federal stack.

Declarative Recruitment was purpose-built to serve prime contractors executing federal and defense technology contracts. We don't generalize. We place AI/ML engineers, software developers, and data scientists into roles where precision and security awareness are non-negotiable.

As a certified small business, we fulfill subcontracting requirements while delivering the caliber of talent that moves contracts forward.

Specialist, not generalist
We focus exclusively on AI/ML and software engineering talent — the exact profiles primes struggle most to hire on contract timelines.
Small business qualified
NAICS 561310 registered. We help primes fulfill small business subcontracting requirements without sacrificing quality.
Network depth in the right places
Built from years placing AI/ML engineers at tier-1 funded companies — talent that is elite, vetted, and increasingly interested in mission-driven work.
