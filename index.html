01 — Who We Are

A technical recruiting partner built for federal program delivery.

Declarative Recruitment specializes in AI/ML, software engineering, data, cloud, and DevSecOps recruiting for organizations delivering federal and defense technology programs.

We support prime contractors, federal technology vendors, HR teams, engineering leaders, and program managers who need technically credible candidates on contract-driven timelines.

Our work is focused on roles where generic recruiting is not enough: hard technical requirements, security-sensitive environments, location constraints, clearance considerations, and high delivery expectations.

As a SAM.gov registered small business, Declarative Recruitment can support prime contractors' subcontracting strategies while delivering focused technical recruiting for the roles that are hardest to fill.

Specialist, not generalist
We focus on AI/ML, software engineering, data, cloud, and DevSecOps roles — the technical profiles federal contractors struggle to hire quickly and accurately.
Built for contract timelines
We understand post-award ramp-ups, active program staffing, surge hiring, recompete transitions, and the pressure to submit qualified candidates quickly.
Clearance-aware from the start

Small-business ready

Commercial AI network depth

05 — Process

A focused process from role intake to candidate slate.

Federal technical hiring fails when recruiters do not understand the role, the contract environment, or the constraints around who can actually support the program.

Our process is built to reduce wasted cycles and deliver candidates your team can evaluate with confidence.

Step 01
Program and Role Intake
We clarify the technical requirements, labor category, clearance needs, citizenship requirements, work location, compensation range, engagement model, and hiring timeline.
OutputSearch profile, candidate criteria, sourcing strategy.
Step 02
Targeted Technical Sourcing
We build a focused search across AI/ML, software engineering, data, cloud, DevSecOps, and federal-adjacent talent networks.
OutputTargeted outreach campaign and qualified candidate pipeline.
Step 03
Clearance-Aware Screening
We screen for current clearance status, clearance eligibility indicators, citizenship requirements, work authorization, geography, availability, compensation alignment, and interest in federal mission work.
OutputCandidates filtered for technical and program fit before submission.
Step 04
Qualified Candidate Slate
You receive a concise slate of candidates with relevant technical background, availability, compensation alignment, location fit, and screening notes.
OutputActionable candidates, not generic resume volume.
Step 05
Interview, Feedback, and Close Support
We coordinate candidate communication, interview flow, feedback loops, offer process, onboarding handoff, and follow-up.
OutputFaster movement from qualified candidate to accepted offer or contract start.
